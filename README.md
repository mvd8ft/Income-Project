# Income Analysis Project

This project uses the Adult Income dataset to analyze whether working more hours is associated with higher income.

## Question
Do people who work more than 40 hours per week tend to earn more than 50K?

## Findings
The analysis shows that individuals who work more than 40 hours per week are more likely to earn above 50K. Additionally, the average working hours for high-income individuals is higher than for low-income individuals.

This suggests that longer working hours may be associated with higher income, although other factors such as education and occupation may also influence income.
